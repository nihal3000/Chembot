But-1-ene: 1 C=C bond, 3 C-C bonds, and 8 C-H bonds
But-2-ene: 1 C=C bond, 3 C-C bonds, and 8 C-H bonds

ΔH = ΔH_f (But-2-ene) - ΔH_f (But-1-ene)
ΔH = (-3.7656 kJ/mol) - (-20.0832 kJ/mol)
ΔH = 16.3176 kJ/mol
