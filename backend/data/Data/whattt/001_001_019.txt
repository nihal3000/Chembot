Naming organic compounds
Identify the IUPAC name of the organic compound with the molecular formula C3H8O.
There are two possible isomers for the molecular formula C3H8O:

1. Propan-1-ol (also known as 1-propanol or n-propyl alcohol): CH3CH2CH2OH
2. Propan-2-ol (also known as 2-propanol or isopropyl alcohol): CH3CH(OH)CH3

Both of these compounds are alcohols with three carbon atoms and one hydroxyl group. The difference lies in the position of the hydroxyl group on the carbon chain.
