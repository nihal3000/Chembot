How many structural isomers are possible for C6H14?
For C6H14 (hexane), there are a total of 5 structural isomers possible. They are:

1. n-hexane (straight chain): CH3-CH2-CH2-CH2-CH2-CH3
2. 2-methylpentane (isohexane): CH3-CH(CH3)-CH2-CH2-CH3
3. 3-methylpentane: CH3-CH2-CH(CH3)-CH2-CH3
4. 2,2-dimethylbutane: CH3-C(CH3)2-CH2-CH3
5. 2,3-dimethylbutane: CH3-CH(CH3)-CH(CH3)-CH3
